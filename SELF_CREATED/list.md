Since your goal is selling landing pages and AI automation services, focus on niches where:

* Customer lifetime value is high
* One lead can be worth ₹50k–₹10L+
* Most businesses have terrible websites
* Owners answer calls directly
* ROI is easy to prove

### Tier 1 (Highest ROI)

1. **Dentists**

   * One patient = ₹5k–₹1L+
   * Easy to show ROI
   * Most websites are outdated
   * Add appointment booking + WhatsApp

2. **Plastic Surgeons / Cosmetic Clinics**

   * One lead can be worth ₹20k–₹5L+
   * Huge margins
   * They spend on marketing

3. **Lawyers**

   * Corporate lawyers
   * Property lawyers
   * Divorce lawyers
   * Immigration lawyers

4. **Real Estate Builders**

   * One sale = lakhs in commission
   * Need project landing pages constantly

5. **Medical Tourism Agencies**

   * Foreign patients coming to India
   * High-ticket leads
