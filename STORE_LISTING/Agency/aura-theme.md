## 🔮 Launch Your AI SaaS Platform in Style

**Aura** is a premium, high-converting HTML landing page template built specifically for AI startups, productivity spaces, and developer services.

Using a futuristic dark aesthetic highlighted by glowing radial gradients, glassmorphic headers, and a live canvas-based floating particles background, Aura makes an incredible first impression. Best of all, it has zero compilation dependencies (no node_modules, no bun setups, no next builds needed!). It's lightweight, loads in milliseconds, and can be customized by editing simple inline parameters.

### Key Highlights

* **Futuristic Dark Theme**: Features custom violet and neon blue gradients, responsive outlines, and glowing icons.
* **Interactive Canvas Particles**: Interactive floating circle animation running smoothly in the background.
* **Monthly/Yearly Pricing Switcher**: Dynamic price cards that animate and update pricing figures using vanilla JS.
* **Infinite Integration Marquee**: Interactive scrolling block displaying logo bindings.
* **No-Compile Setup**: Ready-to-go HTML, Tailwind CDN, and Google Fonts. Double-click or upload to Surge.sh in 3 seconds.
