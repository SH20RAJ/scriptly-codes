## 💎 Long Description

### Stop Sending Boring, Flat Resumes. Start Command Attention.

In a sea of generic PDF resumes and boilerplate bootstrap templates, **KRAFT** is your secret weapon to stand out, command respect, and close high-ticket design and development clients instantly.

KRAFT is a premium digital product that transforms the typical portfolio into an immersive, game-like 3D experience directly in the web browser. The user walks through a beautifully hand-drawn, sketch-style 3D paper corridor. Navigating scroll-linked doors leads to dedicated room experiences for **About**, **Works Gallery**, **Tech Studio**, and a highly creative **Message in a Bottle** contact scene.

Built on React 19, Three.js, React Three Fiber, and GSAP, this theme is visually premium and heavily optimized for 60fps mobile and desktop performance.

---

## ✨ Features That Sell:

*   **🎮 Game-Like 3D Navigation:** An interactive infinite paper corridor that reacts dynamically to user scrolling with parallax split letter effects.
*   **🖼️ Dynamic Works Gallery:** Showcase your premium projects on hand-drawn cards that flip and float seamlessly in three dimensions.
*   **💻 Interactive Monitor Stack:** Display your YouTube channels, blog posts, or TikTok feeds on animated CRT-style rotating screens.
*   **🌊 Immersive Contact Experience:** A fully animated wave-shore where users write their message, roll it up, and throw it into the sea inside a glass bottle.
*   **⚡ Elite WebGL Optimizations:** Zero lag spikes through asynchronous shader compilation (`gl.compileAsync`), baked global shadow lighting, and adaptive device-tiering.
*   **🔍 Invisible Semantic SEO Fallback:** Standard search engine crawl compliance built-in so Google indexers parse your headers, text copy, and articles without loading WebGL canvases.
